FAQ's

Our hackathon is exclusively for college students from all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you're a freshman or a senior, this event is open to you to showcase your talents and creativity.

You can participate either as an individual or as part of a team. We encourage both solo participants and teams to join in the spirit of innovation and collaboration.

To participate effectively, you'll need to bring your own laptop or computer, any specific hardware or software required for your project, chargers, and any personal items you may need during the event. Additionally, it's a good idea to have some form of identification for check-in.

The judging criteria may include innovation, technical complexity, usability, and the impact of the project. Detailed criteria will be provided in the event guidelines, giving you a clear understanding of how projects will be evaluated.
